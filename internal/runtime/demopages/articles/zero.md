# Zero-Knowledge

--embed[alt=Zero-Knowledge,type=image/jpeg,localfilename=articles/img/dm0006.jpg]--

She can prove she knows the secret without ever revealing it, and that is exactly why they want her. A short fictional piece for the dcrpulse pages demo.

--endofpost--

They gave her a room with no windows and one question, asked a hundred ways. Each time she answered the same way: a proof that she knew, and nothing of what she knew.

By morning the interrogators had filled three notebooks and learned exactly nothing, which was the only outcome the mathematics had ever allowed.
