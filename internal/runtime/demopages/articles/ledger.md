# The Ledger of Whispers

--embed[alt=The+Ledger+of+Whispers,type=image/jpeg,localfilename=articles/img/dm0004.jpg]--

In a city where every transaction is watched, a courier carries the one ledger no one can read. A short fictional piece for the dcrpulse pages demo.

--endofpost--

The cafe on Cipher Street kept no ledger of its own, which was the point. Payments passed hand to hand, signed once and forgotten, and the only record that mattered travelled in a courier's coat.

She had memorised the route the way others memorise a seed phrase: twelve turns, no repeats, never the same hour twice.
